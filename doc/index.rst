Welcome to Centreon-GLPI's documentation!
=========================================

The Centreon GLPI module offers the possibility to automatically
import Network equipment and Computers that are declared in the GLPI
software.

Also, alerts (Host down, Service critical etc...) that are raised by
the monitoring system can trigger tickets on GLPI.
